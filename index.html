Who am I ?

I am a novice web-designer. I love to create things from crocheting to making makeshift cardboard fortes on my veranda. I remember once on valentines day in first-grade I made handmade cards with little inscriptions for everyone in my class ;unfortunately they didn't have candy attached to them so they weren't well received . From a very young age I understood the empowerment that comes with creating something with nothing.

I have a belief that learning how to code will help me make more of my ideas a reality. I want to create the best body of work I can and also later to be able to contribute to the programming community.

Goals

I want become adept in building web pages through four major languages:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • PHP

I'd like to use my knowledge to gain some short-term income as a freelance web-designer. In the future I want to leverage those experiences to build my own personal sites and applications that will help people. In addition to, creating a passive income for myself.
